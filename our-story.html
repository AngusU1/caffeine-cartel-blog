How It All Began

The First and only Blog which gives a fuck about great Coffee. None of this pretentiousness often found in coffee culture, a place where you will never be recommended Costa, Starbucks or Blank Street. A community of People sharing the best spots in areas all over the world. From Salento to Edinburgh to London, the misssion is simple, find the best Coffee available.

Founded in 2025, I am on a mission to eliminate expensive and underwhelming coffee experiences. We believed that great coffee should be accessible, enjoyable, and most importantly, shared with others who appreciate the craft.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to spread good quality powder (Caffine), by growing supply lines and identifying the best quality product available.

Through our blog, we share brewing techniques, cafes, reviewing equipment, exploring coffee origins, and celebrating the culture that surrounds this beverage.

Why "Cartel"?

The main mission is built around finding the best brown powder around. The name felt natural.
